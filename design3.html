Visual Analytics Tool for Pokémon Team Recommendation

Pokémon team builder is a visual analytics tool that empower gamers make well-informed decisions about which Pokémon to include in their team based on their preferences.

Project characterization

Alignment of Data and Tasks

Additional data: Type interaction

We added type interaction data to calculate the interaction score between two types. The type interaction mechanism is based on the type chart in Pokémon Database .

  • 1. Filtering: Users can limit the recommender system to specific group of Pokémon
  • 2. Identify: Scatterplot view where the user can find a Pokémon based on their Attack/Defence stats
  • 3. Discover: Being able to find new undiscovered Pokémon for your team with the support of the K-Means clustering algorithm
  • 4. Compare: Radar chart where a user can compare two Pokémon’s stats and compare it to the average stat of all the Pokémons
  • 5. Summarize: Another radar chart that summarizes the teams stats after the recommendation has been made

  • 2. Design a Pokémon card and highlight the most important stats for each Pokémon
