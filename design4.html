ByMySide System

Leveraging Website Object Detection, AR, and ASR & TTS Synthesis to enhance independent and confident mobile app and website experiences for older adults.

In Switzerland, 49% of individuals aged 65 and above own a smartphone

Their smartphone usage mainly centers on basic applications like email, information searches, timetable retrieval, and navigation. Fewer than 3% use mobile apps for activities like buying and selling goods, internet banking, social networks, and internet forums.

Older adults value independence, embrace technology, and aspire to an active lifestyle like the younger generation

Despite potential limitations in motor, vision, and cognitive abilities, similar to teens and their parents, older adults seek apps that assist them in exercising, monitoring their health, shopping, entertaining, and more.

Barriers that prevent older adults from using digital products

Inspiration & ideation

Drawing insights from our recent AI in Active Assisted Living project, where Sina and I conducted expert interviews and qualitative analysis, I was inspired to design a system that enhances independent and confident experience for older adults with up-to-date technologies.

Avoid Stigmatization

"Most interfaces for older adults simply dumb down the functionality of mobile phones and other smart devices."

Provide access to the complete range of device capabilities through “invisible” support, ensuring a seamless user experience without stigmatizing the user.

"Older adults tend to seek a local hotline they could call right then for real-time assistance."

Offer real-time support to aid users in understanding and navigating applications, fostering a sense of confidence and independence.

Real-Time Support

Step-by-Step Navigation

"They prefer guided navigation with clear steps, making it easier for them to access the functions they are looking for."

Guide users with step-by-step instructions, using clear and concise language, and utilize visual cues to enhance the user-friendly experience.

Make confident decisions

Function Explanation and Interaction Gesture

Navigate to specific pages and locate desired functions

Visualized step-by-step guidance

Prevent getting lost inside apps or websites
